This Cookie Policy explains how Kairat-tech.com uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our website at https://www.kairat-tech.com ("Website"). It provides information on what these technologies are, why I use them, and your rights to control our use of them.

In some cases, Kairat-tech.com may use cookies to collect personal information, or this information may become personal if combined with other data.
What are cookies?

Cookies are small data files placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites function, work more efficiently, and provide reporting information.

Cookies set by the website owner (in this case, Kairat-tech.com) are called "first-party cookies." Cookies set by parties other than the website owner are known as "third-party cookies." These cookies allow third-party features or functionalities (such as advertising and analytics) to be provided on or through the website. Third parties can recognize your device both when it visits our website and when it visits other websites.
Why does Kairat-tech.com use cookies?

Kairat-tech.com uses first- and third-party cookies for various reasons. Some cookies are essential for our Website to function properly and are referred to as "essential" or "strictly necessary" cookies. We also use cookies to track and target the interests of users to enhance their experience. Third-party cookies are used for advertising, analytics, and other purposes. This is explained in more detail below.
